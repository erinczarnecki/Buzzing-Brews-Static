Our Story

We serve coffee, but we’re not your typical coffee shop. Inspired by the great cafés of Europe, where conversation blooms and writers, artists, activists and intellectuals make their mark, we’ve made a conscious decision to slow things down. There’s no wifi, no television and no 30-minute maximum for our tables. Come and stay awhile.
